Seattles 3 Best Places To Photograph

Pike Place Market

Pike Place Market is a public market in Seattle that is overlooking the Elliot Bay waterfront. It is one of the longest running farmer markets in the United States and is a place of business for many small farmers, craftspeople and merchants.

Gasworks Park

Gas works Park is a 19 acre public park on the site of the former Seattle Gas Light Company Gastification plant.

Snoqualmie Falls

Snoqualmie falls is a 268 ft waterfall on the Snoqualmie river between Snoqualmie and Fall City.
